Work Experience

  • General Assembly, Chicago West Nile Predictor
    Aug 2018 – Sep 2018

    I cleaned and prepared a 10000+ point dataset from Kaggle with statistics about West Nile Virus in Chicago from 2007-2014. I then constructed Random Forest, Logistic, and Support Vector Classifier models to predict when and where different mosquitos will test positive for West Nile virus.

  • General Assembly, NLP Subreddit Classifier
    Jul 2018 – Aug 2018

    I used the Reddit API to parse 1000 posts each from 2 subreddits. I cleaned and organized data to classify posts on which subreddit each post was from. I used vectorizing, Random Forest, and Gradient boosting to build an NLP classification model which predicted which subreddit a post was most likely from.

  • Faurecia, Acoustic Engineer
    Jun 2016 – Jan 2017

    I was employed to work on a sound quality project to help minimize misunderstandings with clients. I used MatLab to analyze over 100 sound files for calibration using a binaural head that simulated the how a human would hear the sounds. There were a total of around 10000 points of subjective opinions on the sounds. Using ArtemiS and MATLAB, I performed a psychoacoustic study which output a set of plots to map subjective keywords from responses to objective acoustic metrics. I presented my results and helped the company's engineers better understand clients
